# Glossary

**Terms, Expressions and Acronyms (TEA) — sorted alphabetically by TEA (PT)**

| **_TEA_ (PT)** | **_TEA_ (EN)** | **_Description_ (EN)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Administrador do Centro** | **Center Administrator** | Employee responsible for managing and configuring the healthcare center's capacities and settings. |
| **Agendamento** | **Appointment** | A scheduled slot (date and time) for an SNS User to receive a specific type of vaccine. |
| **Centro de Saúde** | **Healthcare Center** | The physical facility where vaccinations take place, defined by its operating hours and hourly capacity. |
| **Enfermeiro** | **Nurse** | Healthcare professional responsible for administering vaccines and recording the vaccination process details. |
| **Funcionário** | **Employee** | General entity representing anyone working at the center, identified by ID, name, and phone number. |
| **Processo de Vacinação** | **Vaccination Process** | The actual record of vaccine administration, including lot number and adverse reactions. |
| **Rececionista** | **Receptionist** | Employee who manages user registration and confirms appointments upon the user's arrival. |
| **Tipo de Vacina** | **Vaccine Type** | A category of vaccines defined by the disease they target (e.g., COVID-19, Influenza). |
| **Utente do SNS** | **SNS User** | A citizen registered in the National Health Service who is eligible to receive vaccinations. |
| **Vacina** | **Vaccine** | The specific commercial product and brand used during the vaccination process. |
